Life Care Hospital - Jaffna

branch focuses on maternal and neonatal care, equipped with modern facilities and experienced professionals to ensure the best care for mothers and infants.Life Care Hospital Jaffna is a leading healthcare facility in the region, offering a wide range of medical services designed to meet the needs of the local community. Life Care Hospital - Vavuniya

Our Vavuniya branch specializes in orthopedic surgery and rehabilitation. With state-of-the-art equipment and expert staff, we provide top-notch care for recovery and rehabilitation. Life Care Hospital Vavuniya is a trusted healthcare institution dedicated to providing top-notch medical services to the people of the region. Strategically located in the heart of Vavuniya, the hospital is committed to improving the health and well-being of the community through its comprehensive range of healthcare services. Offering 24/7 Emergency Care, the hospital is well-prepared to handle any critical medical situation with speed and expertise. Life Care Hospital Vavuniya is equipped with state-of-the-art Diagnostic Imaging Facilities, including MRI, CT scan, X-ray, and Ultrasound, ensuring precise diagnostics and timely treatments. The hospital's Outpatient and Inpatient Services cater to a variety of specialties, allowing patients to receive both routine and specialized medical care under the supervision of experienced healthcare professionals. The hospital excels in Cardiology and Heart Care, providing advanced diagnostic and treatment options for heart-related conditions. In addition, it offers a dedicated Maternity and Neonatal Care Unit, ensuring the safety and well-being of mothers and newborns through all stages of pregnancy and delivery.
