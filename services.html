Our Available Services

At Life Care Hospital, we offer a comprehensive range of healthcare services, each tailored to meet the specific needs of our patients. From emergency care to specialized medical treatments, our dedicated professionals provide quality care every step of the way.

24/7 Emergency Care

Life Care Hospital provides round-the-clock emergency services, ensuring immediate medical attention at any time of the day or night. Our highly trained medical staff is ready to respond to critical situations with speed and efficiency, equipped with the latest technology.

Outpatient and Inpatient Services

We offer comprehensive outpatient and inpatient services, providing personalized care for patients whether they need short-term treatment or extended hospital stays. Our facilities are designed to cater to a wide range of medical conditions with comfort and professionalism.

Advanced Diagnostic Imaging (MRI, CT, X-ray)

Our hospital features state-of-the-art diagnostic imaging services, including MRI, CT scans, and X-rays. These advanced imaging technologies help our medical team provide accurate diagnoses and create effective treatment plans for various health conditions.

Cardiology and Heart Care

Our cardiology department specializes in heart health, offering expert diagnosis, treatment, and preventive care for cardiovascular diseases. From routine check-ups to complex heart surgeries, we are dedicated to providing the best heart care.

Orthopedic Surgery and Rehabilitation

Life Care Hospital excels in orthopedic surgery and rehabilitation, offering cutting-edge treatments for bone, joint, and muscle conditions. Our rehabilitation team works closely with patients to ensure a smooth recovery process.

Maternity and Neonatal Care

We provide comprehensive maternity and neonatal care, ensuring both mother and baby receive the best possible care throughout pregnancy, childbirth, and postnatal recovery. Our facilities are equipped to handle normal deliveries and high-risk pregnancies alike.

Oncology (Cancer Care)

Our oncology department offers advanced cancer care, including chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and surgical oncology. We provide compassionate care for cancer patients with a multidisciplinary approach, tailored to each patientâ€™s specific needs.

General Surgery

Our general surgery department handles a wide range of surgical procedures, from minor operations to complex surgeries. Our skilled surgeons use the latest techniques to ensure the best possible outcomes for patients.

Intensive Care Unit (ICU)

Our ICU is equipped with cutting-edge technology and staffed by experienced critical care specialists, providing intensive monitoring and life-saving treatments for critically ill patients 24/7.

Physical Therapy and Rehabilitation

We offer comprehensive physical therapy and rehabilitation services to help patients recover from injuries, surgeries, and medical conditions. Our dedicated therapists design personalized treatment plans to restore mobility and improve quality of life.

Pharmacy Services

Our in-house pharmacy provides a wide range of medications and offers convenient, quick, and reliable service for all patients. We ensure that prescriptions are filled accurately and efficiently.

Laboratory Services

Life Care Hospital offers full-service laboratory facilities for accurate and timely diagnostic tests. From blood work to specialized testing, our lab plays a crucial role in supporting patient care.

Health Check-up Packages

We offer a variety of health check-up packages tailored to different needs, from routine wellness checks to specialized screenings for specific conditions. These preventive packages help patients stay on top of their health.
